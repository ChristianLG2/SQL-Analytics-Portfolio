# 01 · Vendor Sales Analytics
### T-SQL · Python · pandas · matplotlib · Quarto

Analysis of supplier revenue concentration, category rankings, 
monthly revenue trends, and fulfillment performance across 
the Northwind database.

## Key Findings
- Revenue grew 3.5x over 18 months — $27,861 to $99,415
- Top 5 suppliers account for X% of total net revenue
- Federal Shipping most reliable at 94% on-time
- United Package highest volume (326 orders) but lowest on-time rate (91%)
